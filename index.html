We have a minimal commission, and we pay bonuses for each trip. Some of our competitors keep the bonuses for themselves. We work honestly, and with us, drivers earn more!
High income with guaranteed payments

Work with multiple partners simultaneously

  • We connect you to the Uber system for 10%.
  • No deposits, 0%!
  • We can connect remotely without visiting the office
  • No need for individual entrepreneurship (IE)
  • Orders are distributed without considering the rating of the nearest drivers
  • Work only with cashless payments
  • We transfer money to the driver's card or in cash at the office
Daily earnings range from 7000-12000 UAH + 3000 UAH bonus
TERMS: with company car:
  • We provide the car without a deposit
  • No daily rental payments
  • On Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday, you work for the company, and on other days, you work for yourself
REQUIREMENTS:
  • Car must not have any advertising
  • The car should not be yellow or have yellow plates
  • Car should be no older than 3 years
  • At least 3 years of driving experience
  • Device running Android 4.0 or higher (Samsung)
DOCUMENTS:
  • Driver's license (both sides)
  • Passport (front side)
  • Vehicle registration certificate
  • License (if available)
  • Phone number
  • Bank card number for money transfer (include in the letter)!
